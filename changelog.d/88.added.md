Added `zero_alloc` to `ConversionOptions` and environment toggles for the zero-allocation fast path. `TOONED_HOOK_ZERO_ALLOC=0`, `TOONED_PIPE_ZERO_ALLOC=0`, and `TOONED_WRAP_ZERO_ALLOC=0` switch `tooned hook`, `tooned pipe`, and `tooned wrap` back to the full `maybe_tooned` pipeline so dictionary, entropy, and critical-field tiers can be used when latency is less important.
